Delete Your Account

If you would like to delete your CashMate account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

What Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your email address and login credentials
  • All transaction records (income and expenses)
  • Loan and liability records
  • Portfolio and balance data
  • All other data associated with your account
⚠ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your financial records will be permanently erased. Please export any data you wish to keep before submitting a deletion request.

How to Request Deletion

Send an email to us from the email address associated with your CashMate account with the subject line "Delete My CashMate Account".

Send your request to:

Request Account Deletion

Processing Time

We will process your deletion request within 30 days of receiving it. You will receive a confirmation email once your account and data have been deleted.
