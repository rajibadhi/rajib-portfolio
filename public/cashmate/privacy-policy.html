Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 22, 2026

CashMate ("we," "our," or "us") is a business ledger application developed by Rajib Adhikari. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the CashMate mobile application ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Email Address: Collected during account registration via Firebase Authentication for login and account management purposes.
  • Financial Data: Transaction records, income, and expense entries that you voluntarily enter into the App. This data is stored securely in Firebase Firestore and is associated with your account.
  • Device Identifiers: Automatically generated device and user IDs used for app functionality and analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your user account
  • To store and display your financial records within the App
  • To provide core app functionality such as ledger tracking, reports, and summaries
  • To improve app performance and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely using Google Firebase services. Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS protocols. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Your financial records remain private and are only accessible through your authenticated account.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication: For secure user login and account management
  • Firebase Firestore: For secure cloud data storage

These services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

7. Account Deletion

You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data by visiting our Account Deletion page or by contacting us at the email address below. Upon receiving a deletion request, we will remove all your personal data within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

CashMate is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child under 18 has provided personal information, we will take steps to delete such data promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
